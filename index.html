About Me


Hi, I'm Archie Moon, an eCommerce professional with a passion for helping online businesses thrive via data-driven solutions. Whether it’s refining analytics, improving marketing campaigns, or forecasting future trends, I work with my clients to create strategies that ensure long-term success. Let’s connect and take your e-commerce business to the next level.

Services

Shopify Reporting & Forecasting

I will analyze your Shopify store’s performance to provide actionable insights. From sales trends to customer behavior, I offer custom reports that allow you to make data-driven decisions and optimize your business strategy. I’ll also implement relevant Shopify plug-ins like Klaviyo for personalized email campaigns, Recharge Subscription to boost customer retention, and Triple Whale to consolidate your data and optimize ad performance.

On the forecasting side, I will create auto-populating reporting documents to help predict future trends, manage inventory more effectively, and make informed decisions to prepare for market changes.

Facebook Ads Set-up & Management

I will create Facebook Ad Manager audiences and campaign structures tailored to help you reach your ideal customer. Following initial performance and assessment of your needs, I can continue to monitor and optimise your account longer-term. Using audience segmentation, performance tracking, and AB testing, I will manage your account to ensure your ads are driving conversions and providing a strong return on investment.

Google Ads & Analytics

I will set up and manage your Google Ads campaigns to drive targeted traffic, optimize ad spend, and maximize conversions through precise audience targeting and optimization. By integrating Google Analytics, I’ll track key performance indicators like traffic sources, user behavior, and conversions, providing you with actionable insights to refine your marketing strategies, improve customer engagement, and ultimately boost your ROI.
